# uk-job-market-dissertation
AI-powered interactive data storytelling platform analysing the UK job market (2021-2026) for international students and prospective UK applicants. Covers vacancy trends, visa sponsorship, salary thresholds and regional insights across 5 sectors: Technology, Healthcare, Finance, Engineering and Education.

COMP7039 Dissertation | MSc Data Science and AI | Oxford Brookes University
